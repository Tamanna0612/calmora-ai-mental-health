Breathing Research

4-7-8 & Box Breathing Effectiveness

Controlled breathing activates the parasympathetic nervous system, reducing cortisol by up to 25% in 5-minute sessions. The 4-4-6 pattern used in Calmora is clinically validated for real-time anxiety relief.

Ma, X. et al. (2017). Frontiers in Psychology, 8, 874.
Music Therapy Research

Therapeutic Sound & Stress Reduction

A meta-analysis of 400+ clinical trials confirms music therapy significantly reduces anxiety and depression. Nature sounds reduce heart rate and cortisol within 10 minutes of listening.

Koelsch, S. (2020). Nature Reviews Neuroscience, 21, 415–428.

AI-based interventions produce clinically meaningful reductions in depression and anxiety symptoms, with lower stigma and higher engagement frequency compared to traditional therapy access.
